Grader Notes

  • Use WASD to move and also the arrow keys to move. Q and E are used to rotate left and right. You can press and hold but it might be finicky and in that case, do continuous clicks instead
  • Click the screen to use the perspective camera and esc to stop using it
  • Use the keys P/p to place a block and R/r to remove a block
  • For my wow point, I decided to implement collision detection so that the user can't go through the walls - it's a little finicky as you have to maintain a certain radius from the wall.
  • Story: find the dog in the maze and place blocks that are dog treat wrapped in hopes of bringing the dog back to the user
  • Used ChatGPT to help me create the dynamic maze and also helped me calculate the placement of the dog because I wanted it to be near the end of the maze
