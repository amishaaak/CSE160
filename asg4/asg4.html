Grader Notes

  • In order to move the camera, use the keyboard (WASD/QE), arrow keys, or click on the canvas and drag the mouse
  • The lights off button turns all the lights off while the lights on button turns the lights on
  • To turn on the spotlight and keep the moving light, click the spotlight on button
  • Change the lights color by using a color picker for ambient, diffuse, and specular
  • Change the light position by using the X,Y, Z sliders
